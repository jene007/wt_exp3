Did You Know?

Space is completely silent! There is no atmosphere in space, so sound has no way to travel and be heard. But you can see amazing things like stars, planets, and galaxies!

Biology Rocks!

Some frogs can hibernate for months under frozen ponds—their bodies freeze and then thaw back to life! Nature is so creative and mysterious.
